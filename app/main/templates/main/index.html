Welcome to The Quilting Lilly

Welcome to The Quilting Lilly(TQL). TQL is a homebased quilting service providing 2 kinds of services. The first service is quilting those beautiful tops that are sitting around waiting to be finished. The second is taking a stack of t-shirts and making them into a wonderful keepsake quilt to be cherished.

For the quilting piecer who does want or know how to finish out that top that you’ve put so much work into, I will help you design and finish your quilt. We will discuss quilting patterns and thread choices. I can do simple edge to edge design, semi-custom, or some custom work. I aim for a 3-4 week turn around. Let me help you finish and enjoy those tops that you have spent so much time putting together.

For the person who is looking for a way to turn a stack of cherished t-shirts into a memorable keepsake, I will work with you on what style, quilting pattern, and thread choices you would like. I aim for a 4-6 week turn around. T-shirt quilts make wonderful quilts that have lots of meaning for personal use or as gifts.

I have a 26” computerized quilting longarm by Innova which makes quilting so much fun. I love watching a quilt come to life as it is getting stitched with a pattern to compliment the pieced top and fabric of a quilt. I began quilting about 15 years ago but received my longarm in late 2015. I continue to take classes and learn more about my craft when I have the opportunity. Quilting is a wonderful expression of art with functionality.

Let me help you complete your quilt or create that t-shirt keepsake.
